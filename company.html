Our Company

  • We are a group of professionally qualified engineers with cumulative experience of over 9 decades in industry.
  • We are into providing plant engineering services and automation solutions for polymer compounding industry for the last 25 years .
  • We have served compounding industry in various segments like cable compound, masterbatch, engineering plastics and PVC compounding .
  • We have also made attempt to leverage our understanding of compounding equipment by providing start up and after sales services .
  • We are authorized service partners for Krauss Maffei Extrusion formerly Krauss Maffei Berstorff , Elin motoren .
  • Now we are in strategic partnership with Innovent Technologies AG, Switzerland for services in various specialized gear boxes .
  • We are in advance stage of negotiations for services with European companies for their post extrusion equipment
